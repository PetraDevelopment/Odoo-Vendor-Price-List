Last Date Purchasing

This module is used to find out the latest price on the last date you purchased from this vendor.

1- When creating a quotation purchase from any supplier at a price and date.

2- Product Name > On the Purchase tab > there is a new column called Latest Purchase.

3- In Purchase > Vendor > there is a new tab called Product for each vendor with the products you purchased from him with the last date and last price.

4- When you buy the same product, for example, from the same vendor but with a different product date and price, “Auto Update” will appear in the vendor and product.

5- In Product > Purchase tab > The product appears with the new price and date.

6- In Vendor > New Product tab > The product appears with the last updated price and date.
